Welcome!

This dashboard is a prototype designed to explore ways the Metadata Services Unit (MSU) can share data related to metadata production and quality.

What's new?

  • A foray into visualizing connections between metadata production and digital imaging. Check out "MSU/DIU in context" under Metadata production to see the relationships between metadata creation, the addition of digital images to the repository, and metadata approvals. You'll also be able to view the top collections contributing to the statistics for each month.
  • Improved research library division selection: it's now easier to select a research center, multiple research divisions, all divisions, or a single division

Is the data live?

No. The data is static.
