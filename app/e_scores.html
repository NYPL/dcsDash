Metadata quality comparison

We assessed the six metadata elements above required for the minimum viable description of digitized items. This view compares scores between the end of March 2019 and the end of June 2019.

  • Green is a passing score
  • Yellow is a partial score (i.e. some but not all of the quality criteria were met)
  • Orange is a failing score (i.e. the element is not present at the item-level)

Why is this important?

This information provides a more granular analysis to help us strategize and track targeted remediation projects. Improving metadata quality improves discovery of and context for our online collections.
